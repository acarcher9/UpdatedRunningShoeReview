Running Shoe Reviews

Written By Runners, For Runners

We are a team of running enthusiasts, volunteers, amateurs, professional athletes, and coaches who want to help you find the best shoe for you. This blog will cover the basics of picking a shoe, care, and when it's inevitably time to find your new pair.

How to Get Involved

We invite submissions for our blog! Please get in touch to write for the blog. We also host an anual 5K and 10K to benefit our town's local shelters and put it on with 100% volunteer support! We look forward to teaming up with you.

Notice of Transparency

Our contributors do not receive payment for their work. This is a labor of love, much like running! Sometimes brands send us samples to test, but we will always disclose this information and we will never receive a portion of any sales of products we review. We hope that this site is informative, inclusive, and community-focused.
